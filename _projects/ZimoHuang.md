Zimo joined the lab after graduating with an MSci in Neuroscience from UCL and was a research assistant in the lab from 2024-2026. Her research focussed on analysing MEG and intracranial EEG data sets to understand the neural mechanismsm of goal-directed spatial navigation in humans. She left the lab to pursue a PhD with Sven Bestmann at the UCL Institute of Neurology.
