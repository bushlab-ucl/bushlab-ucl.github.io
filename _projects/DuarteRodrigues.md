Duarte is a part-time PhD student in the lab, who spends the remainder of his time working with [Neroes](https://neroes.tech/) - a neurotechnology startup developing an AI-enhanced gamified neurofeedback platform. Duarte holds both a BSc and an MSc in Biomedical Engineering from the University of Porto, and his MSc research was conducted in collaboration with the [Laboratory of Cognitive Neuroscience at EPFL](https://www.epfl.ch/labs/lnco/), where he investigated behavioural patterns associated with presence hallucinations in Parkinson's disease. His PhD research investigates how closed-loop stimulus presentation and neurofeedback can be used to enhance memory performance.
