Since I was five, I've wanted to understand how periodic structures like grid cells and prefrontal circuits organise thought and how defects in these systems lead to cognitive breakdown in conditions such as schizophrenia. Thanks to my supervisors Izaak Neri, Daniel Bush, and Rick Adams, I'm now doing exactly that.
